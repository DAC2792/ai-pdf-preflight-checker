## Issues Found

### 1. 🖼️ Low Image Resolution — Pages 1 & 2
**What's wrong:** The images in this file are only 51 DPI (dots per inch). Print requires a minimum of 300 DPI. At this resolution, your printed images will appear heavily pixelated and blurry.

**Fix:** Replace all images with high-resolution versions at 300 DPI or above before exporting your PDF.

---

### 2. ✂️ No Bleed — Pages 1 & 2
**What's wrong:** Your file has no bleed (0mm on all sides). Bleed is a small extra border of artwork that extends beyond the trim edge. Without it, you risk getting white edges on your finished printed item.

**Fix:** Extend your background colours and images by at least 3mm beyond the page edge on all sides, then re-export your PDF.

---

### 3. 🔤 Fonts Not Embedded — Pages 1 & 2
**What's wrong:** The fonts used (Helvetica and Helvetica Bold) are not embedded in the file. If our print systems don't have these fonts installed, your text may change appearance or fail to display correctly.

**Fix:** When exporting your PDF, ensure the "Embed All Fonts" option is selected. In most design applications this is found in the PDF export settings.

---

### 4. 🎨 Wrong Colour Profile — Page 1
**What's wrong:** Your file is set to RGB colour mode, which is designed for screens. Print uses CMYK. Colours can shift unexpectedly when converted at our end, meaning your final print may look different to what you see on screen.

**Fix:** Convert your document to CMYK colour mode in your design application before exporting the final PDF.
