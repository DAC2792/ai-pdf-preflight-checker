## Issues Found

### ❌ 1. Missing Bleed — Pages 1 & 2

**What's the problem?**
Your file has no bleed (0mm on all sides). Bleed is a small border of extra artwork that extends beyond the edge of your design. Without it, the printer has no margin for error when cutting, and you risk ending up with thin white edges on your finished product.

**How to fix it:**
Extend your background colours and images by at least **3mm beyond the edge** of your design on all sides, then export the PDF again with bleed included.

**What's the problem?**

**What's the problem?**
Your image resolution is approximately **51 DPI**, which is very low. For print, you need at least **300 DPI**. At 51 DPI, images will appear blurry and pixelated when printed — what looks acceptable on screen will look poor in real life.

**How to fix it:**

**What's the problem?**

**How to fix it:**
